Lemonade

Description:

Lemonade is a sweetened lemon-flavored drink.

Ingredients:

  • White Sugar - 1 3/4 cup
  • Water - 8 cups
  • Lemons - 9
  • Ice

Steps:

  1. Combine the sugar along with 1 cup of water in a saucepan. Stir to dissolve, and heat until boiling.
  2. Roll lemons on a flat surface to soften. Cut in half and squeeze into a container, be sure to remove any seeds that come out. Juice until you have 1 1/2 cups of juice
  3. Pour in the remaining 7 cups of water into a pitcher, then add in lemon juice and the sugar mixture. Add ice.
